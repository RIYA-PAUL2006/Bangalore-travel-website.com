Commercial Street

Location:

Shivaji Nagar

Highlight:

A bustling shopping hub known for affordable fashion, accessories, and street food. Popular among college students and tourists for its vibrant atmosphere.

Brigade Road

Location:

MG Road

Highlight:

Famous for its mix of branded stores, local shops, pubs, and eateries. It's a prime spot for street shopping and nightlife.

Church Street

Location:

Off MG Road

Highlight:

Known for its cafes, bookstores, and art galleries. A lively spot for street photography and cultural exploration.

Chickpet Market

Location:

KR Market

Highlight:

One of Bangalore's oldest markets, renowned for sarees, textiles, and jewelry at wholesale prices.

KR Market

Location:

Near Chickpet

Highlight:

A colorful market filled with fresh flowers, spices, and traditional goods. Great for experiencing the local culture.

MG Road

Location:

Central Bangalore

Highlight:

A high-energy area offering street shopping, entertainment, and food stalls. A blend of modern and traditional Bangalore.

Jayanagar 4th Block

Location: South Bangalore


Highlight:

A shopper's paradise for traditional wear, books, and home decor. The area also has popular street food vendors.

Koramangala 5th Block

Location:

South-East Bangalore

Highlight:

Famous for its trendy street cafes, boutique stores, and buzzing nightlife. A favorite among young crowds.

Avenue Road

Location:

Near Chickpet

Highlight:

Known for its second-hand bookstores and stationery shops, Avenue Road is a treasure trove for book lovers.

VV Puram Food Street

Location: Near Lalbagh


Highlight:

A must-visit for street food enthusiasts. Offers traditional Karnataka snacks like dosa, idli, and sweets.
