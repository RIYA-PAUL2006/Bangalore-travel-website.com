Libraries

"Bangalore's libraries: where history meets knowledge, inspiring minds one book at a time."

Temples

State Central Library
(Seshadri lyer Memorial Hall)

Location: Cubbon Park


Highlights:

  • Iconic red building surrounded by lush greenery.
  • Houses over 3 lakh books, including rare manuscripts.
  • Well-organized sections for students, researchers, and casual readers.

Indian Institute Of Science(IISC) Library

Location: IISc Campus, Malleshwaram


Highlights:

  • Advanced research facilities for science and technology enthusiasts.
  • Digital resources and journals available.
  • Access restricted to IISc members and researchers with prior permission

Bangaalore University Library

Location: Jnana Bharathi Campus, Mysore Road


Highlights:

  • Extensive academic resources for students and researchers.
  • E-library and digitized book collections.
  • Ideal for pursuing higher education projects.

British Council Library(BCL)

Location: Prestige Takt, MG Road


Highlights:

  • Rich collection of English literature, educational materials, and international journals.
  • Offers membership for physical and digital resources.
  • Frequent literary and cultural events.

JustBooks Library

Location: Multiple branches across Bangalore (e.g., HSR Layout, Whitefield)

The Hindu Newspaper Library

Location: Kasturi Buildings, MG Road


Highlights:

  • Ideal for journalists, media students, and history enthusiasts .
  • Archival access to decades of The Hindu publications.
  • Offers resources for media research.

Eloor Library

Location: Residency Road


Highlights:

  • Specializes in fiction, academic texts, and rare books.
  • Offers convenient borrowing options with a large inventory.
  • Peaceful reading environment

National Law School Library

Location: Nagarbhavi


Highlights:

  • Focused on law and legal studies
  • Houses national and international law journals.
  • Access limited to law students and researchers

The Indian Institute Of Management Bangalore (IIMB) Library

Location: Bannerghatta Road


Highlights:

  • Renowned for its collection of business, finance, and management resources.
  • Modern facilities with access to online databases and journals .
  • Open to IIMB students and registered scholars .
.

Karnataka State Archives Library

Location: Vidhana Soudha, Ambedkar Veedhi


Highlights:

  • Houses government records, ancient manuscripts, and official documents.
  • Useful for historical and administrative research.
  • Open for public use with prior approval.
